MASALA DIAMOND

INGREDIENTS:
  • All Purpose Flour / Maida - 1 cup
  • Ajwain / Carom Seeds / Omam - ½ tsp
  • Cumin Seeds / Jeerakam - ½ tsp
  • Asafoetida / Hing / Kaya Podi - a pinch
  • salt to taste
  • oil for deep frying
  • Water as needed
  • Chat masala-optional
    • STEPS:
    1. Take flour, salt, chilli powder, ajwain, cumin, asafoetida in a bowl. Mix well.
    2. Add in water and mix well.
    3. Heat a tsp of oil till smoking and pour that into the dough and knead well.
    4. Rest it for 10 mins. Now divide it into equal portions.
    5. Take a dough and roll it into a thin sheet..The sheet should be really thin.
    6. Now use a pizza cutter or a knife and cut diamonds from it
    7. Separate them.
    8. Heat oil for deep frying and drop these in hot oil.. Fry till golden.
    9. Drain it into a bowl, sprinkle with chat masala if needed.
    10. Serve
